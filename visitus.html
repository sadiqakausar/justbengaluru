The Bangalore palace : It has been known for its beauty ever since. The awe inspiring Gothic windows and woodcarvings bear testimony to the stunning architecture of the era passed.

The Tipu summer palace: It is representative of the rich art forms and artistic talent that existed in that reign.The two-storied structure has pillars, balconies and carved arches.

The Iskcon Temple :It has deities of Radha and Krishna located at Rajajinagar, in North Bangalore, Karnataka, India. It is one of the largest ISKCON temples in the world.
